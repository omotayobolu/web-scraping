Project title: DESIGN AND IMPLEMENTATION OF A COMPUTERISED REFERENCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM 
Project Abstract: ABSTRACT The paper examines the design and implementation of a computerized reference management system. The research project was carried out to solve the referencing/plagiarism problem students undertaking their research projects in universities, polytechnics and colleges of education face. The research project covered four (4) major referencing styles namely; MLA, APA, HARVARD and CHICAGO.
