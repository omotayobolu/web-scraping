Project title: DESIGN AND IMPLEMENTATION OF A COMPUTERIZED FOOD MENU DIRECTORATE SYSTEM 
Project Abstract: ABSTRACT The main objective of this study is to develop an ICT assisted menu directorate system, whereby someone could be able to choose a meal, get the list of ingredients to cook the meal and follow a step by step help in order to cook the meal. In the other hand, it will also consist of a food time table, where certain meal will be designated for a certain time. ...
