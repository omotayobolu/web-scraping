Project Abstract: ABSTRACT Mobile Number portability (MNP) is a platform that enables subscribers to seamlessly switch mobile telecommunications service providers without changing their phone numbers. MNP gives the subscribers the privilege to easily change from networks that are not serving them well and also return when the network improves ensuring greater flexibility and mobility across networks.
